Scrambled Eggs


Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is the recipe of deliciously rich scrambled eggs.


Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method


  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 secs
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from heat
